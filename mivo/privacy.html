Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 10, 2026

🔒 Our Privacy Commitment

Melovo ("we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy. We are committed to protecting your data and maintaining transparency. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our music player application.

1. Introduction

Melovo is a music player application focused on simple playback, playlist management, and a smooth listening experience. By using Melovo, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect and Process

Melovo does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data or usage data from your device. The app is designed to operate entirely on-device:

  • All data stays on your device. Your music library, playlists, playback history, settings, and preferences are stored locally using the device's standard local storage. They are never uploaded to our servers or to any third party.
  • No usage or analytics data is collected. We do not track what songs you play, how long you listen, which screens you visit, or any other usage behavior.
  • No device identifiers are collected. We do not access the IDFA (Identifier for Advertisers), device ID, advertising ID, or any persistent device identifier.
  • No crash reports are sent automatically. The app does not include any crash reporting SDK.

Because Melovo collects nothing, there is no personal information, payment data, contacts, location, microphone, or camera data being collected, used, or transmitted by us.

3. Third-Party Content

Melovo may access music, cover artwork, lyrics, or related media from third-party content providers or public sources. When you interact with this content:

  • Music and related media may be provided by third-party services or public sources.
  • We do not sell your personal information to these third-party platforms.
  • These platforms have their own independent privacy policies, and we recommend reviewing them to understand their data practices.

4. Third-Party Services & Data Collection

Melovo does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, tracking, or crash reporting SDK. Specifically:

  • No analytics services (such as Firebase Analytics, Google Analytics, Sentry, Amplitude, or Mixpanel) are integrated.
  • No advertising networks (such as Google AdMob, Meta Audience Network, or Unity Ads) are integrated.
  • No user tracking SDKs are integrated. The app does not use the AppTrackingTransparency framework because it does not track users.
  • No crash reporting or performance monitoring SDKs are integrated.

When the app fetches public music-related metadata (such as cover artwork or lyrics) from public sources, such requests are made only to retrieve publicly available content and do not include your personal data or device identifiers.

5. Data Security

We take data security seriously. Although we do not collect your personal data, we ensure that all network requests (e.g., for fetching music lists or covers) are transmitted via encrypted secure protocols (HTTPS/TLS). Please note that while we strive to protect your info, no transmission over the internet can be guaranteed as 100% secure.

6. Children's Privacy

Melovo is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently processed such information, please contact us immediately.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights:

  • Stop Data Transmission: You can limit future data interaction by uninstalling the application.
  • Contact Us: For any privacy-related questions, you can reach out to us directly.
