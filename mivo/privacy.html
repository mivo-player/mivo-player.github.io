Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 26, 2026

🔒 Our Privacy Commitment

Melovo ("we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy. We are committed to protecting your data and maintaining transparency. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our music player application.

1. Introduction

Melovo is a music player application focused on simple playback, playlist management, and a smooth listening experience. By using Melovo, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect and Process

Melovo is designed to minimize data collection. Depending on how you use the app, we may process limited information such as:

  • Usage and Technical Data: We may receive crash diagnostics, analytics events, device model, operating system version, and app performance data through our service providers.
  • Locally Stored Preferences: Playback history, app settings, and similar preferences may be stored on your device.

We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal information, payment card details, contacts, precise location data, microphone recordings, or camera recordings for core app operation.

3. Third-Party Content

Melovo may access music, cover artwork, lyrics, or related media from third-party content providers or public sources. When you interact with this content:

  • Music and related media may be provided by third-party services or public sources.
  • We do not sell your personal information to these third-party platforms.
  • These platforms have their own independent privacy policies, and we recommend reviewing them to understand their data practices.

4. Third-Party Services & Data Collection

To improve our service, we may use third-party service providers that may process limited data:

  • App diagnostics and analytics services: Used to understand app stability, performance, and usage patterns.
  • Content and network service providers: Used to retrieve music lists, cover artwork, lyrics, or other app content.

These services may process information such as your device model, OS version, app usage statistics, and network request metadata in accordance with their own privacy policies.

5. Data Security

We take data security seriously. Although we do not collect your personal data, we ensure that all network requests (e.g., for fetching music lists or covers) are transmitted via encrypted secure protocols (HTTPS/TLS). Please note that while we strive to protect your info, no transmission over the internet can be guaranteed as 100% secure.

6. Children's Privacy

Melovo is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently processed such information, please contact us immediately.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights:

  • Stop Data Transmission: You can limit future data interaction by uninstalling the application.
  • Contact Us: For any privacy-related questions, you can reach out to us directly.
